General Conduct

Be on Time

Participants must be at designated venue at least 30 minutes prior to scheduled start time. Latecomers will be disqualified.

Provided Kit

Projects to be submitted for the competition must only be created using the components and provided by the designated robotics kit.

Remain Inside

Participants must remain within the competition area once the event has begun. Participants who will leave during the event will be disqualified.

No Talking

During the event, participating groups must not communicate with each other. Any attempt to do so will lead to disqualification.

Authority

The organizers have the authority to modify the rules as needed and disqualify teams for undisclosed reasons or take alternative measures.

Rules

Not knowing the rules doesn't excuse breaking them. If you have any questions about the rules, please reach out to our support.

Teams will be created either in random order or based on the participants' preferences. All teams must consist of at least 3-4 members. Participants who do not have a team upon registration will be assigned to a team by the organizers.
Each team will be given a team name and must work together throughout the competition. Participants may request to change teams prior to the start of the competition, but the organizers have the authority to deny such requests.
